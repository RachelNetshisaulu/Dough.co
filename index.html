Our story

Baked with
obsessive care.

Dough started in a small Johannesburg kitchen with a simple belief: exceptional ingredients, handled with skill and love, produce cakes that people remember long after the last slice is gone.

Every cake is made to order — never pre-made, never stored. We source our butter from local dairies, our flour stone-ground, and our seasonal fruit from nearby farms whenever possible.

Whether it's a six-tier wedding cake or a single birthday slice, each creation gets the same unhurried attention.
